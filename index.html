Stammering

Stammering, also known as stuttering, is a speech disorder characterized by disruptions in the normal flow of speech, such as repetitions, prolongations, or blocks.

Articulation Disorders

Articulation disorders involve difficulty in producing speech sounds correctly, affecting the clarity of speech. This can be due to incorrect placement, timing, or flow of movements of the lips, tongue, or throat.

MORE
02

Fluency Disorders

Fluency disorders, such as cluttering or rapid speech, are characterized by disruptions in the natural flow or rhythm of speech. This can include repetitions, interjections, or revisions that make speech difficult to understand.
